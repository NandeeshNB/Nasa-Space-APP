Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

        
        1. What is EcoLens?
            EcoLens is a team of storytellers, data scientists, and environmental advocates 
            dedicated to transforming complex climate data from the US Greenhouse Gas Center 
            into engaging, actionable insights.
            

        2. How do you use data from the US Greenhouse Gas Center?
            We analyze emissions data and create visual stories that help the public understand 
            the sources, impacts, and solutions for greenhouse gases.
            
            
        3. Why is visualizing greenhouse gas data important? 
            Visualizing data makes the complex issue of climate change more accessible and relatable,
             helping people see the effects and potential solutions more clearly.
            
            
        4. What kinds of stories does EcoLens tell? 
            We focus on the causes and impacts of greenhouse gases, highlighting solutions to reduce
             emissions and showcasing the carbon footprint of everyday actions.
            
            
        5. Who is EcoLens for?
            EcoLens is for individuals, educators, communities, and policymakers who want to better 
            understand climate change and take informed action.
